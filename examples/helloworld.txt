my name is equal to the answer of "What is your name?".

age is equal to ask question "What is your age?" as number.

show "You are "age" years old!".

if age is lower or equal to 4 then:
    display "You are the youngest person ever!".
else if age is greater or equal to 18 then:
    say "You are an unc!".
else:
    say "You are a minor.".
end.
